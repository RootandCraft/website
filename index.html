Meet Your Host

Hello, I'm Cass

Root & Craft was born from the belief that creativity is for everyone — and that creating with your hands is a simple, nourishing way to reconnect with yourself and the present moment.


I'm Casandra, the founder and host of Root & Craft. I create intimate, thoughtfully curated workshops in Vancouver where small groups of people come together to be present, create, and connect — in a space that feels as good as it looks.

Cass

The Root & Craft Experience

More than a workshop

Every detail is intentionally considered — from curated snacks and herbal teas to the finest materials and tools, thoughtfully prepared for you. You'll leave with something you've created by hand, and a sense of connection that lingers beyond the experience.

  • All materials and tools provided
  • Healthy snacks, filtered water, coffee, and herbal tea included
  • Beginner-friendly — no experience needed
  • Small groups for an intimate, relaxed atmosphere
  • Take home your handmade items in thoughtfully prepared packaging
  • Leave with renewed energy and the satisfaction of creating something beautiful by hand
