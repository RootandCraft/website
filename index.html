Meet Your Host

Hello, I'm Cass

Root & Craft was born from a deep belief that creativity is for everyone — and that making something with your hands is one of the most nourishing things you can do for your soul.


I'm Casandra, the founder and host of Root & Craft. I create intimate, thoughtfully curated workshops in North Vancouver where small groups of women come together to learn, make, and connect — in a space that feels as good as it looks.

The Root & Craft Experience

More than a workshop

Every detail is considered — from the curated snacks and herbal teas to the tools laid out just so. You'll leave with something you made with your own hands, and hopefully a few new friends too.

  • All materials and tools provided
  • Snacks, filtered water & herbal tea included
  • Beginner-friendly — no experience needed
  • Small groups for an intimate, relaxed atmosphere
  • Take home your handmade earrings in a gift box
  • North Vancouver, BC location
